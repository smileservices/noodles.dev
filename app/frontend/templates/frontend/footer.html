Why Noodles?

We know how difficult is to learn software engineering. It's a steep learning curve and the threshold required for getting a job in the industry is pretty high. Therefore, many students have to live economically and eat the cheapest food there is: instant noodles!
We believe that noodles is a symbol for the empowered student that fights through messy lines of code and confusing bugs. Never loose hope! That's why we have noodles here.
